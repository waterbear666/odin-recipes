Chocolate Paper

This rare delight is served by accident to the sneakiest good boys.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate cupcakes
  • Or Trader Joes Dark Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups

Steps

  1. Eat your chocolate treat of choosing in bed
  2. Become so sloth like, that you leave all of the paper wrappings from your chocolate treat on your bedside table
  3. Wake up in the morning to find your good boy has eaten all your trash for you
  4. Worry if you need to take your good boy to the vet
