Peanut Butter Bowl

The Good Boy favorite! This dish can be utilized for the purpose of grooming, calming, luring or distracting your goodest boy.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Trader Joe's Greek Yogurt with Honey

Steps (E.g. Luring)

  1. Combine yogurt and peanut butter in a medium bowl
  2. Eat your snack in bed
  3. Lure your good boy to bed with the smell of peanut butter
  4. When finished, hold bowl up to your good boy and rotate for maximum tongue coverage
  5. Snuggle your good boy
